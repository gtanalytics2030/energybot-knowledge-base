## CHUNK 07 — Rate LTP (Lift Irrigation — Low Tension)

**DOCUMENT:** Annexure: Tariff Schedule of Four State Owned DISCOMs (DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL & UGVCL) — FY 2026-27
**DOCUMENT TYPE:** Tariff Schedule
**HIERARCHY:** Part I — Low and Medium Voltage Tariffs — Rate LTP
**PROVISION:** Rate LTP — Lift Irrigation, Clause 6
**TITLE:** Rate LTP — Lift Irrigation (LT Agricultural, up to 180 HP, 24-hour supply) — DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL, UGVCL
**ISSUING AUTHORITY:** Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC)
**EFFECTIVE DATE:** 1st April 2026
**SUMMARY:** Rate LTP applies to Low Tension Agricultural consumers with contracted load up to 180 HP who require continuous (24-hour) power supply for lifting water from surface water sources (canal, river, or dam) and supplying water directly to farmers' fields for agricultural irrigation only. Fixed charges are Rs. 20/- per HP per month. Energy charges are 80 paise/unit for entire monthly consumption. This category is distinct from the general agricultural Rate AG.
**KEY TOPICS:** Lift irrigation, LT agricultural, surface water, canal, river, dam, agricultural irrigation, fixed charges, energy charges, 24-hour supply, continuous supply, LTP

**TEXT:**

**6. RATE: LTP- LIFT IRRIGATION**

Applicable for supply of electricity to Low Tension Agricultural consumers contracting load up to 180 HP requiring continuous (twenty-four hours) power supply for lifting water from surface water sources such as canal, river, & dam and supplying water directly to the fields of farmers for agricultural irrigation only.

| Charge Head | Rate |
|---|---|
| (a) Fixed charges per month | Rs. 20/- per HP |
| (b) Energy charges per month: For entire consumption during the month | 80 Paise per Unit |

**REFERENCED PROVISIONS:**
- Rate AG (excludes installations covered under LTP – Lift Irrigation category)

## CHUNK 08 — Rate WWSP (Water Works and Sewerage Pumping)

**DOCUMENT:** Annexure: Tariff Schedule of Four State Owned DISCOMs (DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL & UGVCL) — FY 2026-27
**DOCUMENT TYPE:** Tariff Schedule
**HIERARCHY:** Part I — Low and Medium Voltage Tariffs — Rate WWSP
**PROVISION:** Rate WWSP, Clauses 7.1 to 7.4
**TITLE:** Rate WWSP — Water Works and Sewerage Pumping (LT, Three Types) — DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL, UGVCL
**ISSUING AUTHORITY:** Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC)
**EFFECTIVE DATE:** 1st April 2026
**SUMMARY:** Rate WWSP covers three categories of water works and sewerage pumping at LT. Type I (non-local authority): fixed Rs. 25/HP/month; energy 430 paise/unit post-paid, 417 paise/unit pre-paid. Type II (Municipal Corporation, GWSSB outside Gram Panchayat): fixed Rs. 20/HP/month; energy 410 paise/unit post-paid, 398 paise/unit pre-paid. Type III (Municipalities, Nagarpalikas, Gram Panchayats, and GWSSB in Gram Panchayat areas): energy only at 320 paise/unit post-paid, 310 paise/unit pre-paid (no fixed charge shown). All WWSP consumers with ToD/smart meters get a 40 paise/unit off-peak discount during 1100–1800 hrs.
**KEY TOPICS:** Water works, sewerage pumping, local authority, Municipal Corporation, GWSSB, Gram Panchayat, Nagarpalikas, off-peak discount, Time of Use, fixed charges, energy charges, Type I, Type II, Type III, WWSP

**TEXT:**

**7. RATE: WWSP**

This tariff shall be applicable to services used for water works and sewerage pumping purposes.

**7.1. Type I — Water works and sewerage pumps operated by other than local authority:**

| Charge Head | Post-Paid Energy Charge | Pre-paid Energy Charge |
|---|---|---|
| (a) Fixed charges per month | Rs. 25/- per HP | Rs. 25/- per HP |
| (b) Energy charges per month: For entire consumption during the month | 430 Paise per Unit | 417 Paise per Unit |

**7.2. Type II — Water Works and sewerage pumps operated by local authority** such as Municipal Corporation, Gujarat Water Supply & Sewerage Board located outside Gram Panchayat Area will also attract this tariff:

| Charge Head | Post-Paid Energy Charge | Pre-paid Energy Charge |
|---|---|---|
| (a) Fixed charges per month | Rs. 20/- per HP | Rs. 20/- per HP |
| (b) Energy charges per month: For entire consumption during the month | 410 Paise per Unit | 398 Paise per Unit |

**7.3. Type III — Water Works and sewerage pumps operated by Municipalities, Nagarpalikas, Gram Panchayats and Gujarat Water Supply & Sewerage Board for its installations located in Gram Panchayats:**

| Charge Head | Post-Paid Energy Charge | Pre-paid Energy Charge |
|---|---|---|
| Energy charges per month: For entire consumption during the month | 320 Paise per Unit | 310 Paise per Unit |

**7.4. TIME OF USE DISCOUNT:**

Applicable to all the water works Consumers with meters having ToD recording facility / Smart Meter.

| Period | Discount |
|---|---|
| For energy consumption during the off-peak period, viz. 1100 Hrs. to 1800 Hrs. | 40 Paise per Unit |

**REFERENCED PROVISIONS:**
- General Clause 15 (ToU charges on ToD-equipped meters)

## CHUNK 09 — Rate AG (Agricultural)

**DOCUMENT:** Annexure: Tariff Schedule of Four State Owned DISCOMs (DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL & UGVCL) — FY 2026-27
**DOCUMENT TYPE:** Tariff Schedule
**HIERARCHY:** Part I — Low and Medium Voltage Tariffs — Rate AG
**PROVISION:** Rate AG, Clauses 8.1 to 8.4
**TITLE:** Rate AG — Agricultural (Irrigation, excluding LTP Lift Irrigation) — DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL, UGVCL
**ISSUING AUTHORITY:** Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC)
**EFFECTIVE DATE:** 1st April 2026
**SUMMARY:** Rate AG applies to electricity supply for irrigation purposes, excluding installations under LTP-Lift Irrigation. Three billing options: (1) HP-Based Tariff at Rs. 200/HP/month flat (all-in); (2) Metered Tariff with fixed charges of Rs. 20/HP/month plus **60 paise/unit** energy charges (a heavily subsidised agricultural rate for state DISCOMs); (3) Tatkal Scheme at Rs. 20/HP/month fixed plus 80 paise/unit, eligible for transition to normal metered tariff after five years from commencement of supply. Only water pump for irrigation (plus a single bulb/CFL up to 40W) is permitted; other machinery requires a separate connection at appropriate tariff. Agricultural consumers supplying water to brick manufacturing units must pay Rs. 100/HP/annum (minimum Rs. 2000/year per brick unit) in addition to normal tariff, declared and paid in advance before the financial year (in March).
**KEY TOPICS:** Agricultural tariff, irrigation, HP-based tariff, metered tariff, Tatkal scheme, pump motor, brick manufacturing, separate connection, advance declaration, fixed charges, energy charges, 60 paise, subsidised agricultural rate

**TEXT:**

**8. RATE: AG**

This tariff is applicable to services used for irrigation purposes only excluding installations covered under LTP- Lift Irrigation category.

**8.1. The rates for following group are as under:**

**8.1.1. HP BASED TARIFF**

| Charge Head | Rate |
|---|---|
| For entire contracted load | Rs. 200 per HP per month |

**ALTERNATIVELY**

**8.1.2. METERED TARIFF**

| Charge Head | Rate |
|---|---|
| Fixed Charges | Rs. 20 per HP per month |
| Energy Charges: For entire consumption during the month | **60 Paise per Unit** |

**8.1.3. TATKAL SCHEME**

| Charge Head | Rate |
|---|---|
| Fixed Charges | Rs. 20 per HP per month |
| Energy Charges: For entire consumption during the month | 80 Paise per Unit |

**NOTE:** The consumers under Tatkal scheme shall be eligible for normal metered tariff as above, on completion of five years period from the date of commencement of supply.

**8.2.** No machinery other than pump water for irrigation (and a single bulb or CFL up to 40 watts) will be permitted under this tariff. Any other machinery connected in the installation governed under this tariff shall be charged separately at appropriate tariff for which consumers shall have to take separate connection.

**8.3.** Agricultural consumers who desire to supply water to brick manufacturing units shall have to pay Rs. 100/HP per annum subject to minimum of Rs. 2000/- per year for each brick Mfg. Unit to which water is supplied in addition to existing rate of HP based / metered agricultural tariff.

**8.4.** Such Agricultural consumers shall have to pay the above charges for a full financial year irrespective of whether they supply water to the brick manufacturing unit for full or part of the Financial Year. Agricultural consumers shall have to declare their intention for supply of the water to such brick manufacturing units in advance and pay charges accordingly before commencement of the financial year (i.e. in March every year).

**REFERENCED PROVISIONS:**
- Rate LTP — Lift Irrigation (excluded from Rate AG scope)
- General Clause 16 (delayed payment at 12% p.a. for AG category)

## CHUNK 10 — Rate TMP (Temporary Supply, LT)

**DOCUMENT:** Annexure: Tariff Schedule of Four State Owned DISCOMs (DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL & UGVCL) — FY 2026-27
**DOCUMENT TYPE:** Tariff Schedule
**HIERARCHY:** Part I — Low and Medium Voltage Tariffs — Rate TMP
**PROVISION:** Rate TMP, Clauses 9.1 to 9.2 and Note
**TITLE:** Rate TMP — Temporary Supply at Low Voltage — DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL, UGVCL
**ISSUING AUTHORITY:** Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC)
**EFFECTIVE DATE:** 1st April 2026
**SUMMARY:** Rate TMP applies to electricity supply for a temporary period at low voltage. A consumer not taking supply on a regular basis under a proper agreement is deemed a temporary supply consumer. Fixed charges are Rs. 15 per kW per day per installation. Energy charges are a flat 465 paise/unit. Bills must be paid within seven days of issue; supply is disconnected on 24 hours' notice for non-payment.
**KEY TOPICS:** Temporary supply, LT temporary, fixed charges per day, flat energy rate, payment within 7 days, disconnection on non-payment, 24-hour notice, TMP

**TEXT:**

**9. RATE: TMP**

This tariff is applicable to services of electricity supply for temporary period at the low voltage. A consumer not taking supply on regular basis under a proper agreement shall be deemed to be taking supply for temporary period.

**9.1. FIXED CHARGE:**

| Charge Head | Rate |
|---|---|
| Fixed Charge per Installation | Rs. 15 per kW per Day |

**9.2. ENERGY CHARGE:**

| Charge Head | Rate |
|---|---|
| A flat rate of | 465 Paise per Unit |

**Note:** Payment of bills is to be made within seven days from the date of issue of the bill. Supply would be disconnected for non-payment of dues on 24 hours' notice.

**REFERENCED PROVISIONS:**
- General Clause 4 (billing period, monthly basis)
- General Clause 16 (delayed payment charges)

## CHUNK 11 — Rate LT EV Charging Stations

**DOCUMENT:** Annexure: Tariff Schedule of Four State Owned DISCOMs (DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL & UGVCL) — FY 2026-27
**DOCUMENT TYPE:** Tariff Schedule
**HIERARCHY:** Part I — Low and Medium Voltage Tariffs — Rate LT EV
**PROVISION:** Rate LT Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Stations, Clauses 10.1 to 10.4
**TITLE:** Rate LT EV Charging Stations — Exclusive Electric Vehicle Charging — DGVCL, MGVCL, PGVCL, UGVCL
**ISSUING AUTHORITY:** Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC)
**EFFECTIVE DATE:** 1st April 2026
**SUMMARY:** Rate LT EV applies to consumers who use electricity exclusively for Electric Vehicle Charging installations. Other consumers may charge EVs under their existing regular LT category. Fixed charges are Rs. 25 per installation per month. Energy charges are 410 paise/unit post-paid and 398 paise/unit pre-paid. Peak ToU charges of 45 paise/unit apply during 0700–1100 and 1800–2200 hrs for installations with contract demand above 10 kW. A 60 paise/unit Time of Use discount applies during 1100–1700 hrs for smart meter / ToD-equipped consumers.
**KEY TOPICS:** Electric vehicle charging, EV station, LT EV, fixed charges, energy charges, ToU peak charges, Time of Use discount, smart meter, ToD meter, contract demand

**TEXT:**

**10. RATE: LT ELECTRIC VEHICLE (EV) CHARGING STATIONS**

This tariff is applicable to consumers who use electricity exclusively for Electric Vehicle Charging installations.

Other consumers can use their regular electricity supply for charging electric vehicle under same regular category i.e. RGP, RGP (RURAL), GLP, LTMD, etc. as the case may be.

**10.1. FIXED CHARGES:**

| Charge Head | Rate |
|---|---|
| Fixed Charge | Rs. 25 per Installation per Month |

**10.2. ENERGY CHARGES: FOR THE ENTIRE MONTHLY CONSUMPTION:**

| Charge Head | Post-Paid Energy Charge | Pre-paid Energy Charge |
|---|---|---|
| Energy Charge | 410 Paise per Unit | 398 Paise per Unit |

**10.3. TIME OF USE CHARGES FOR CONSUMERS HAVING CONTRACT DEMAND ABOVE 10 KW:**

| Period | Charge |
|---|---|
| Additional Charge for energy consumption during two peak periods, viz. 0700 Hrs. to 1100 Hrs. and 1800 Hrs. to 2200 Hrs. | 45 Paise per Unit |

**10.4. TIME OF USE DISCOUNT**

Concession of 60 Paise per Unit shall be applicable for the consumption of energy during 1100 Hrs. to 1700 Hrs. for the Consumers having contract demand up to and including 10 kW with Smart Meter and for the Consumers having contract demand above 10 kW with meters having ToD recording facility / Smart Meter.

**REFERENCED PROVISIONS:**
- Rate RGP, Rate RGP (Rural), Rate GLP, Rate LTMD (applicable to EV charging under regular category)
- General Clause 15 (ToU charges on ToD-equipped meters)
