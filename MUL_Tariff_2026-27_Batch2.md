## CHUNK 12 — Rate 9: HTMD-I (Regular HT Supply)

**DOCUMENT:**
Tariff Schedule for License Area of MPSEZ Utilities Ltd. (MUL) — FY 2026-27

**DOCUMENT TYPE:**
Tariff Schedule / Tariff Order (Annexure)

**HIERARCHY:**
Tariff Schedule → Part-II (HT Supply) → Rate 9

**PROVISION:**
Part-II, Rate 9 — HTMD-I

**TITLE:**
HT Regular Supply Tariff (HTMD-I) — Fixed Charge, Energy Charge, Power Factor Adjustment, EHV Rebate, Minimum Bill

**SUMMARY:**
HTMD-I applies to HT consumers contracting for 100 kVA and above for regular supply not covered under other HT categories. Two sub-tiers apply based on contract demand: up to 500 kVA (fixed charge 75 paise/unit; energy charge 410 paise/unit) and above 500 kVA (fixed charge 110 paise/unit; energy charge 450 paise/unit). Excess demand charges are 125 paise/unit (up to 500 kVA CD) and 150 paise/unit (above 500 kVA CD). Billing demand is the highest of actual MD, 85% of CD, or 100 kVA. Power factor rebates/penalties apply at 90% PF benchmark. EHV rebate on energy charges: 0% at 11 kV, 1% at 33 kV, 2% at 66 kV and above.

**KEY TOPICS:**
HTMD-I, HT supply, regular supply, 100 kVA and above, contract demand tiers, 500 kVA threshold, billing demand, fixed charge, energy charge, power factor rebate, power factor penalty, EHV rebate, minimum bill, 11 kV, 33 kV, 66 kV

**TEXT:**
PART-II — SUPPLY DELIVERED AT HIGH VOLTAGE
(11000 VOLTS AND ABOVE — THREE PHASE, 50 HERTZ)
The following tariffs are available for supply at high tension for contract demand not less than 100 kVA.

9. RATE: HTMD-1
This tariff is applicable for supply of energy to High Tension consumers contracting for maximum demand of 100 kVA and above for regular power supply and requiring the power supply for the purposes not specified in any other HT categories.

9.1 FIXED CHARGE
A) For the Billing Demand of customer having:
a. Contract demand up to 500 kVA
Computed on 85% of contract demand at u.p.f or actual maximum demand at monthly average power factor or one hundred kVA at u.p.f. whichever is higher on monthly basis at 100% Load Factor — 75 Paise per Unit

b. Contract demand above 500 kVA
Computed on 85% of contract demand at u.p.f or actual maximum demand at monthly average power factor whichever is higher on monthly basis at 100% load factor — 110 Paise per Unit

B) For Billing Demand in excess of the Contract Demand:
a. Contract demand up to 500 kVA
Computed on billing demand in excess of Contract Demand on Monthly basis at 100% Load Factor — 125 Paise per Unit

b. Contract demand above 500 kVA
Computed on billing demand in excess of Contract Demand on Monthly basis at 100% Load Factor — 150 Paise per Unit

NOTE: The Billing Demand shall be highest of the following:
i. Actual Maximum Demand established during the month OR
ii. Eighty-five percent of the Contract Demand OR
iii. One hundred kVA.

9.2 ENERGY CHARGE
For entire consumption during the month:
- Up to 500 kVA of the contract demand — 410 Paise per unit
- Above 500 kVA of the contract demand — 450 Paise per unit

9.3 POWER FACTOR ADJUSTMENT CHARGE
A) Where the average Power Factor during the Billing Period exceeds 90%:
- For each 1% improvement in the Power Factor from 90% to 95%: Rebate of 0.15 Paise per Unit
- For each 1% improvement in the Power Factor above 95%: Rebate of 0.27 Paise per Unit

B) Where the average Power Factor during the Billing Period is below 90%:
- For each 1% decrease in the Power Factor below 90%: Penalty of 3.00 Paise per Unit

9.4 REBATE FOR SUPPLY AT EHV

| Sl. No. | Supply Voltage | Rebate on Energy Charges |
|---------|---------------|--------------------------|
| 1 | 11 kV | 0.0% |
| 2 | 33 kV | 1.0% |
| 3 | 66 kV and above | 2.0% |

9.5. MINIMUM BILL
Payment of fixed charges as specified in 9.1 above.

Rebates are available to consumers maintaining PF above 90%, with incremental rates for the 90–95% band and above 95%. A penalty of 3.00 paise/unit per 1% drop below 90% is levied. ⭐ EHV Voltage Rebate — Master Reference (All HT Categories)

**SUMMARY:**
This anchor chunk consolidates the Extra High Voltage (EHV) rebate provisions applicable to HT tariff categories. Consumers availing supply at higher voltages receive a rebate on energy charges: 0% at 11 kV, 1% at 33 kV, and 2% at 66 kV and above. The rebate is available on a monthly basis only. For HTMD-III, HTMD-IV, and Railway Traction, consumers with arrears are not eligible for the rebate; however, consumers whose arrears have been stayed by an appropriate authority or court remain eligible.
