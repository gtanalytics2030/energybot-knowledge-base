## CHUNK 9 — Rate: LTMD-2 (General Non-Residential above 15 kW — LT)

DOCUMENT:
Tariff Schedule for Ahmedabad–Gandhinagar License Area of Torrent Power Limited – Ahmedabad

DOCUMENT TYPE:
Tariff Schedule

HIERARCHY:
Part-I (LT/MV Supply) > Rate: LTMD-2

PROVISION:
Rate: LTMD-2 — Clauses 6, 6.1, 6.2, 6.3

TITLE:
Rate: LTMD-2 — General Non-Residential LT Tariff above 15 kW (and optional for NON-RGP consumers)

SUMMARY:
Rate LTMD-2 applies to LT premises not covered under any other LT category with connected load above 15 kW. It is also available as an optional alternative for consumers under Rate: NON-RGP. Billing demand is the highest of: actual maximum demand, 85% of contract demand, or 6 kW. Fixed charges: Rs. 175/kW (first 50 kW), Rs. 230/kW (next 30 kW), Rs. 300/kW (rest) within contract demand; Rs. 425/kW for excess. Energy charges: 480 paise/unit (billing demand up to 50 kW); 500 paise/unit (above 50 kW). Power factor rebate: 0.15 paise/unit per 1% improvement from 90–95%; 0.27 paise/unit above 95%. Penalty: 3 paise/unit per 1% drop below 90%.

KEY TOPICS:
LTMD-2, LT Maximum Demand, Non-Residential, General Purpose LT, Billing Demand, Contract Demand, Fixed Charge, Energy Charge, Power Factor Adjustment, NON-RGP Option

TEXT:
6. RATE: LTMD-2

Applicable for supply of electricity to premises which are not covered in any other LT tariff categories, having above 15 kW of connected load.

This tariff shall also be applicable to consumer covered in category- 'Rate: Non-RGP' so opts to be charged in place of 'Rate: Non-RGP' tariff.

6.1. FIXED CHARGE

A. For Billing Demand up to and including Contract Demand
(a) First 50 kW of Billing Demand per month — Rs. 175 per kW
(b) Next 30 kW of Billing Demand per month — Rs. 230 per kW
(c) Rest of Billing Demand per month — Rs. 300 per kW

B. For Billing Demand in excess of the Contract Demand
Fixed Charge per kW of Billing Demand per month — Rs. 425 Per kW

Note: The Billing Demand will be taken as under:
i. The Maximum Demand recorded during the month OR
ii. 85% of the Contract Demand OR
iii. 6 kW
Whichever is the highest.

6.2. ENERGY CHARGE
(a) For Billing Demand up to and including 50 kW — 480 Paise per unit
(b) For Billing Demand above 50 kW — 500 Paise per unit

6.3. POWER FACTOR ADJUSTMENT CHARGE

A. Where the average Power Factor during the Billing period exceeds 90%
For each 1% improvement in the Power Factor from 90% to 95% — Rebate of 0.15 Paise per Unit
For each 1% improvement in the Power Factor above 95% — Rebate of 0.27 Paise per Unit

B. Where the average Power Factor during the Billing period is below 90%
For each 1% decrease in the Power Factor below 90% — Penalty of 3.00 Paise per Unit

REFERENCED PROVISIONS:
Rate: NON-RGP (optional shift to LTMD-2); Part-I General Conditions

## CHUNK 13 — Rate: HTMD-1 (General HT/EHT Consumers, 100 kW and above)

DOCUMENT:
Tariff Schedule for Ahmedabad–Gandhinagar License Area of Torrent Power Limited – Ahmedabad

DOCUMENT TYPE:
Tariff Schedule

HIERARCHY:
Part-II (HT/EHT Supply) > Rate: HTMD-1

PROVISION:
Rate: HTMD-1 — Clauses 10, 10.1, 10.2, 10.3, 10.4, 10.5

TITLE:
Rate: HTMD-1 — High Tension / Extra High Tension General Consumers (Minimum 100 kW Demand)

SUMMARY:
Rate HTMD-1 is the primary HT/EHT tariff for general consumers (other than local authority pumping stations) contracting for 100 kW and above. Billing demand is the highest of: actual maximum demand, 85% of contract demand, or 100 kW. Fixed charges within contract demand: Rs. 260/kW for billing demand up to 1000 kW; Rs. 335/kW for 1000 kW and above. Excess demand: Rs. 385/kW. Energy charges: 455 paise/unit (first 400 units per kW of billing demand per month); 445 paise/unit (remaining). Time of Use (TOU) surcharge applies during 0600–0800 hrs and 1700–2300 hrs: 80 paise/unit (billing demand up to 300 kW); 100 paise/unit (above 300 kW). Solar Hour Rebate of 30 paise/kWh applies during 1100–1500 hrs (meter with time-segment feature required). Power factor rebate: 0.15 paise/unit (90–95%); 0.27 paise/unit (above 95%); penalty: 3 paise/unit per 1% below 90%.

KEY TOPICS:
HTMD-1, High Tension, Extra High Tension, HT Consumer, Maximum Demand, Billing Demand, Contract Demand, Fixed Charge, Energy Charge, TOU, Time of Use Charge, Peak Hours, Solar Hour Rebate, Power Factor Adjustment, 11 kV, EHT

TEXT:
PART-II
SUPPLY DELIVERED AT HIGH VOLTAGE AND EXTRA HIGH VOLTAGE
(11000 VOLTS AND ABOVE- THREE PHASE, 50 HERTZ)

10. RATE: HTMD-1

Applicable for supply of energy to High Tension consumers contracting for maximum demand of 100 kW and above for purposes other than pumping stations run by local authorities.

10.1. FIXED CHARGE

A. For Billing Demand up to and including Contract Demand
Fixed Charge per kW of Billing Demand per Month for Billing demand up to 1000 kW — Rs. 260 per kW
Fixed Charge per kW of Billing Demand per Month for Billing demand 1000 kW and above — Rs. 335 per kW

B. For Billing Demand in excess of the Contract Demand
Fixed Charge per kW of Billing Demand per month — Rs. 385 per kW

Note: The Billing Demand will be taken as under:
i. The Maximum Demand recorded during the month OR
ii. 85% of the Contract Demand OR
iii. 100 kW
Whichever is the highest.

10.2. ENERGY CHARGE
(a) First 400 units consumed per kW of Billing Demand per Month — 455 Paise per unit
(b) Remaining Units consumed per Month — 445 Paise per unit

10.3. TIME OF USE (TOU) CHARGE
For the Consumption during specified hours as mentioned here below:
0600 Hrs. to 0800 Hrs. & 1700 Hrs. to 2300 Hrs.
(a) For Billing Demand up to 300 kW — 80 Paise per Unit
(b) For Billing Demand Above 300 kW — 100 Paise per Unit

10.4. SOLAR HOUR REBATE
The energy consumed during Solar hours between 1100 hours and 1500 hours recorded by the tariff meter having built in feature of time segments, shall be eligible for rebate at the rate of 30 Paise per kWh.

10.5. POWER FACTOR ADJUSTMENT CHARGE

A. Where the average Power Factor during the Billing period exceeds 90%
For each 1% improvement in the Power Factor from 90% to 95% — Rebate of 0.15 Paise per Unit
For each 1% improvement in the Power Factor above 95% — Rebate of 0.27 Paise per Unit

B. Where the average Power Factor during the Billing period is below 90%
For each 1% decrease in the Power Factor below 90% — Penalty of 3.00 Paise per Unit

Rate: HTMD-3 — Temporary High Tension Supply (Minimum 100 kW)

Rate HTMD-3 applies to HT consumers taking electricity on a temporary basis (not under a regular agreement) at 100 kW and above.
