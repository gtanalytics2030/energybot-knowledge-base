**TITLE:**
General Conditions of Tariff Application — Scope, Exclusions, Billing Basis, Purpose of Use, GERC Regulations, and Unit Conversion

**SUMMARY:**
These general conditions establish the foundational rules governing the tariff schedule. The tariff rates are applicable to all consumers of TPL-D in the Dahej SEZ area and are exclusive of Electricity Duty, taxes, and other government levies. Supply is restricted to one point per connection, charges are billed monthly, and power must be used only for the purpose for which it is taken. GERC miscellaneous charges regulations (except Meter Charges) continue to apply. The conversion factor for electrical appliances is 0.746 kW = 1 BHP.

GENERAL CONDITIONS

1. The tariff figures indicated in this tariff schedule are the tariff rates payable by all the consumers of Torrent Power Limited – Distribution in the Dahej SEZ area.

2. These tariffs are exclusive of Electricity Duty, tax on sale of electricity, taxes and other charges levied by the Government or other competent authorities from time to time which are payable by the consumers, in addition to the charges levied as per the tariff.

**TITLE:**
General Conditions — Billing Rules, Contract Demand, Fuel Adjustment, Penal Charges, Power Factor, and Delayed Payment

**SUMMARY:**
These conditions govern the mechanics of billing and compliance. Fixed charges are billed in multiples of 0.5 HP/kW, with fractions rounded up; energy charges are billed per complete kWh. Connected load for billing means the maximum load during the billing period. Fixed, minimum, and demand charges are not adjusted for broken periods caused by connection or disconnection. Contract Demand is the maximum kW the licensee undertakes to supply. Fuel Cost and Power Purchase Adjustment Charges follow the GERC-approved formula. Payment of penal charges for excess demand does not confer a right to draw excess power, and payment of power factor penalty does not excuse non-compliance. Delayed payment charges are 15% per annum from the due date; no charges apply if paid within 10 days of billing.

General Condition 16 — Green Power Tariff

**TITLE:**
Green Power Tariff — Rate, Eligibility, and Opt-In Procedure

**SUMMARY:**
A Green Power Tariff of Rs. 0.75 per kWh is levied over and above the normal tariff of the respective consumer category for consumers who opt to meet their energy demand through renewable energy (RE). All consumers — whether at Extra High Voltage, High Voltage, or Low Voltage — are eligible. A consumer wishing to opt in must provide written notice to the Distribution Licensee before the commencement of the relevant billing period, within the Billing Cycle notice period.

**PROVISION:**
Part-I, Paras 1.0 to 1.3 — Rate: RGP

**TITLE:**
Rate RGP — Residential General Purpose Tariff (Low/Medium Voltage, up to 150 kVA)

**SUMMARY:**
Part-I covers tariffs for supply at Low and Medium Tension for contract demand up to 150 kVA. The RGP tariff applies to all residential premises. Single-phase supply covers aggregate loads up to 6 kW; three-phase supply applies above 6 kW. Fixed charges range from Rs. 15 to Rs. 70 per month depending on connected load (BPL consumers pay Rs. 5/month). Energy charges are slab-based: 215, 245, 310, and 400 Paise per unit for successive consumption bands. BPL consumers receive a concessional rate of 150 Paise/unit for the first 50 units, and must produce the BPL card at the zonal office to avail the benefit. The minimum bill equals the fixed charge.
