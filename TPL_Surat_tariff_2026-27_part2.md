PROVISION:
Part-I, Rate 1.0, 1.1, 1.2 — Rate: RGP (Residential General Purpose)

TITLE:
Rate RGP — Residential Tariff (LT): Fixed Charges and Energy Charges for General and BPL Consumers

SUMMARY:
The RGP tariff applies to all residential premises on LT supply (230/400V, contract demand up to 150 kVA). Single-phase supply covers aggregate loads up to 6 kW; three-phase applies above 6 kW. Fixed charges are Rs. 25/month (single-phase) and Rs. 65/month (three-phase) for general consumers, and Rs. 5/month for BPL consumers. Energy charges are slabwise for general consumers (320 to 505 Paise/unit) and concessional for BPL consumers (150 Paise/unit for first 50 units, then at residential rates). BPL consumers must present proof of BPL card; the concession is limited to 50 units per month.

KEY TOPICS:
- Residential tariff (RGP)
- LT supply (230/400V)
- Single-phase vs three-phase supply
- BPL (Below Poverty Line) consumers
- Fixed charges (residential and BPL)
- Energy charges slabs (residential)
- BPL concessional tariff (first 50 units)
- Contract demand up to 150 kVA

TEXT:
PART-I
RATE SCHEDULE – LOW/MEDIUM TENSION
230/400 VOLTS

The following tariffs are available for supply at Low and Medium Tension for Contract demand up to 150 kVA.

1.0 Rate: RGP
This tariff is applicable to all services in the residential premises.

Single-phase supply: Aggregate load up to 6 kW
Three-phase supply: Aggregate load above 6 kW

1.1 Fixed Charges:
For other than BPL consumers
  a. Single Phase Supply — Rs. 25 per installation per month
  b. Three Phase Supply — Rs. 65 per installation per month

For BPL household consumers*
  Fixed Charges — Rs. 5.00 per installation per month

PLUS

1.2 Energy Charges: For the total monthly consumption:
For other than BPL consumers
  (a) First 50 units — 320 Paise per Unit
  (b) Next 50 units — 365 Paise per Unit
  (c) Next 150 units — 425 Paise per Unit
  (d) Above 250 units — 505 Paise per Unit

For BPL household consumers*
  (a) First 50 units — 150 Paise per Unit
  (b) For remaining units — Rates as per Residential

*The consumer who wants to avail the benefit of the above tariff has to produce a copy of the Card issued by the authority concerned at the zonal office of the Distribution Licensee. The concessional tariff is only for 50 units per month.

REFERENCED PROVISIONS:
- General Conditions (Applicability, Billing, Broken Period — Items 1–12)
- General Condition Item 14 (FPPAS applicable)
- General Condition Item 16 (Statutory levies exclusive)

TITLE:
Rate GLP — Government/Municipal/Charitable/Public Premises Tariff (LT): Applicability, Fixed Charges and Energy Charges

SUMMARY:
The GLP tariff applies to lights, fans, heating, general load, and motive power in specified public and charitable premises including crematoriums, government and municipal hospitals, registered charitable institutions (hospitals, schools, research institutes, hostels, religious premises) exempted from general tax, public street lights, and municipal water/sewerage pumping. Halls, gardens, or portions let out commercially must be charged at Non-RGP tariff. Fixed charges are Rs. 55/month per installation; energy charges are a flat 405 Paise/unit.

- Fixed charges: Rs. 55/month
- Energy charges: 405 Paise/unit

- Income Tax Act, 1961 (Section 9)

TITLE:
Rate Non-RGP — LT Commercial/General Tariff (Up to 15 kW Connected Load): Fixed Charges and Energy Charges

The Non-RGP tariff applies to all premises not covered by any other LT tariff category, with connected load up to and including 15 kW. Annexure: Tariff Schedule > Part-I (LT) > Rate: LTMD

PROVISION:
Part-I, Rate 4.0, 4.1, 4.2, 4.3 — Rate: LTMD (Low Tension Maximum Demand)

TITLE:
Rate LTMD — LT Maximum Demand Tariff (Connected Load Above 15 kW): Demand Charges, Energy Charges, Reactive Energy Charges, and Billing Demand Definition

SUMMARY:
The LTMD tariff applies to LT premises not covered by other LT categories with connected load above 15 kW, and is also available optionally to Non-RGP consumers. Demand charges are tiered: Rs. 115/kVA/month (up to 20 kVA), Rs. 155/kVA/month (20–60 kVA), Rs. 225/kVA/month (above 60 kVA), and Rs. 250/kVA/month for excess over contract demand. Billing demand is the highest of actual maximum demand, 85% of contract demand, or 6 kVA. Energy charges are 485 Paise/unit. Reactive energy charges of 10 Paise/kVARh apply for installations of 40 kVA contracted load and above.

KEY TOPICS:
- LTMD tariff (Low Tension Maximum Demand)
- Connected load above 15 kW
- Demand charges (tiered by kVA)
- Billing demand definition (highest of: actual MD, 85% CD, 6 kVA)
- Energy charges: 485 Paise/unit
- Reactive energy charges: 10 Paise/kVARh (for 40 kVA and above)
- Excess contract demand penalty: Rs. 250/kVA/month
- Optional for Non-RGP consumers

TEXT:
4.0 Rate: LTMD
This tariff is applicable for supply of electricity to premises which are not covered in any other LT tariff categories, having connected load above 15kW. This tariff shall also be applicable to consumer covered in category- 'Rate: Non-RGP' so opts to be charged in place of 'Rate: Non-RGP' tariff.

4.1 Demand charges:
  a. Up to 20 kVA of billing demand — Rs. 115/- per kVA per month
  b. Above 20 kVA & up to 60 kVA billing demand — Rs. 155/- per kVA per month
  c. Above 60 kVA of billing demand — Rs. 225/- per kVA per month
  d. In excess of the contract demand — Rs. 250/- per kVA per month

Note: BILLING DEMAND: Billing demand during the month shall be the highest of the following:
  i. Maximum demand recorded during the month
  ii. 85% of the contract demand
  iii. 6 kVA

PLUS

4.2 Energy charges:
  Energy Charges — 485 Paise/Unit

PLUS

4.3 Reactive Energy Charges (kVARh Units):
For installation having contracted load of 40 kVA and above
  For all the reactive units drawn during the month — 10 Paise/kVARh

REFERENCED PROVISIONS:

- General Conditions Items 1–16

Part-I, Rate 6.0, 6.1, 6.2 — Rate: AGP (Agriculture Power)

TITLE:
Rate AGP — Agricultural / Irrigation Motive Power Tariff (LT): Fixed Charges, Energy Charges, and Conditions

SUMMARY:
The AGP tariff applies to motive power services used for irrigation purposes. Fixed charges are Rs. 20 per HP per month; energy charges are 60 Paise/unit. Agricultural consumers are permitted one bulb or CFL up to 40 watts in the pump house at no extra charge; any further load addition constitutes unauthorized extension. No machinery other than an irrigation pump is permitted under this tariff.

KEY TOPICS:
- AGP tariff (Agricultural General Purpose)
- Irrigation motive power
- Fixed charges: Rs. 20/HP/month
- Energy charges: 60 Paise/unit
- Pump house lighting allowance (1 bulb/CFL up to 40W, no charge)
- Unauthorized extension (additional load)
- No non-pump machinery permitted

TEXT:

6.1 Fixed Charges:
  Fixed Charges — Rs. 20 per HP per month

PLUS

  Energy Charges — 60 Paise/Unit
